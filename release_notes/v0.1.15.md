## What's new in v0.1.15

Fixes a stale-snapshot bug where items that had moved out of view on the server (e.g. closed and bumped to Released by a project automation) kept appearing in the menu-bar list with their old status, sometimes for hours.

### Fixes

- **Project items now drop out of the list when the board shrinks.** The streaming-project sync uses a parallel-cursor pagination strategy that re-fetches multiple pages at once. When the project shrunk between syncs (items moved to a status excluded by your filter, archived, or removed), one of the parallel branches would finish earlier than expected and the sync would return without ever telling the frontend "this was the last page." The frontend's prune step is gated on that signal, so stale items lingered in the cached snapshot indefinitely. The pagination layer now correctly marks the true last page as final regardless of where it lands in the parallel batch, plus a safety-net terminal event covers the remaining edge cases (empty trailing page, broken cursor chain). End result: when an item leaves the board, it leaves your list on the next refresh.
