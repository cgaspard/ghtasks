## What's new in v0.4.1

Makes the **Inbox** a full mirror of github.com/notifications.

### Fixes

- **The Inbox now shows every notification**, not just issues and PRs. CI-activity (workflow runs), releases, discussions, and other notification types previously never appeared — which could leave the Inbox looking empty if most of your notifications were CI or "subscribed" updates. It now mirrors github.com/notifications fully. Notifications without a specific issue/PR (like a CI run) link to the repo.
- **CI-activity notifications now trigger a desktop alert** (e.g. a failed workflow run), in addition to review requests, mentions, and replies.

### Install

This release is delivered automatically to anyone running v0.1.11+ — look for the update banner. Manual downloads:

- **macOS:** `GH.Tasks_universal.dmg` (signed + notarized)
- **Windows:** `GH.Tasks_x64-setup.exe` or `GH.Tasks_x64_en-US.msi`
- **Linux:** `GH.Tasks_amd64.AppImage`, `GH.Tasks_amd64.deb`, or `GH.Tasks-1.x86_64.rpm`

Upgrade in place — nothing to reconfigure. (Clickable notifications and sound are macOS features and require the signed app bundle.)
