### Fixes

- **Clicking a notification now opens the item on GitHub.** Tapping a desktop notification opens the underlying issue or pull request in your browser, instead of popping open the GH Tasks window. Straight to the thing you were pinged about.
- **No more stray Dock icon.** Clicking a notification could promote GH Tasks from a menu-bar-only app to a Dock-visible one. It now stays in the menu bar where it belongs.
- **Notifications group by repository.** Multiple pings from the same repo now collapse into a single expandable stack in Notification Center — with one place to clear them — instead of piling up as separate alerts.

### Note on dismissing notifications

Whether a notification shows as a **Banner** (fades on its own) or an **Alert** (stays until you dismiss it) is a macOS setting, not something the app controls. If yours stay on screen, switch it under **System Settings → Notifications → GH Tasks → Banners**.

### Install

Download `GH.Tasks_universal.dmg` (signed + notarized), open it, and drag GH Tasks to Applications. Your sources, token, and settings carry over from any earlier version.
