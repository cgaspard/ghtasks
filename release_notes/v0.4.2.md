## What's new in v0.4.2

Fixes a bug where new notifications could silently never alert you.

### Fixes

- **Fixed a bug where genuinely-new notifications could silently never trigger a desktop alert.** The very first time the app checked your inbox (e.g. right after installing or updating), it correctly avoided flooding you with a notification for every existing unread item — but if that same first check also contained the notifications that should have started ringing going forward (for example, two CI-activity alerts landing at once), they were marked "already seen" without ever notifying, and would never notify later either. The app now distinguishes "this is truly the first check" from "there was simply nothing to report last time," so new items always get a notification once a baseline exists.

  **Note:** if you already installed v0.4.0 or v0.4.1, any notifications that were silently swallowed by this bug won't retroactively fire — only notifications that arrive after upgrading to v0.4.2 are covered by the fix.

### Install

This release is delivered automatically to anyone running v0.1.11+ — look for the update banner. Manual downloads:

- **macOS:** `GH.Tasks_universal.dmg` (signed + notarized)
- **Windows:** `GH.Tasks_x64-setup.exe` or `GH.Tasks_x64_en-US.msi`
- **Linux:** `GH.Tasks_amd64.AppImage`, `GH.Tasks_amd64.deb`, or `GH.Tasks-1.x86_64.rpm`

Upgrade in place — nothing to reconfigure. (Clickable notifications and sound are macOS features and require the signed app bundle.)
